We strongly encourage you to take advantage of your own legal future to the extent possible.

If you are looking for information on a particular issue, here is where you should start:

Mendocino County Ordinances http://www.co.mendocino.ca.us/planning/ordinances.htm

Ukiah City Ordinances http://www.cityofukiah.com

California Codes https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes.xhtml

Mendocino Superior Court Rules http://www.mendocino.courts.ca.gov

General Legal Research http://www.findlaw.com

Judicial Council Forms http://www.courts.ca.gov/forms.htm

Complaints about attorneys http://www.calbar.ca.gov/state/calbar/calbar_home.jsp

Complaints about Judges http://www.cjp.ca.gov

Remember the Mendocino County Law Library is also available to help you. Visit them at https://www.co.mendocino.ca.us/lawlib/
