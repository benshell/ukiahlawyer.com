As in any case, you should carefully evaluate an attorney before retaining them, look at the quality of their work as well as their fees, ask them how charges will be based, and how do they itemize charges in detail. If possible, try to get a reference from a satisfied client who has already used them. Try to get assurances that they will finish a job that they start in a timely manner.
